Project Overview


The Sales Report Generator automates the process of generating business reports from sales data stored in PostgreSQL. 
The pipeline extracts sales records from the database, performs aggregations using Pandas, generates daily and monthly sales reports,
identifies top-selling products, and exports the reports as CSV files.
This project simulates a common reporting workflow used by Data Engineers and Data Analysts where raw transactional data is transformed into business-ready reports.
